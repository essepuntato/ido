#example_en On the 15th of December 2015, a particular stock keeping unit of a certain item, proposed for the season Autumn-Winter 2015, has COGS set to 15 euros, sell-in set to 24 euros, and sell-out set to 39 euros - all prices are net and include VAT. By the end of the year, 5 pieces of this stock keeping unit were sold: two of them were acquired by a customer (with sell-out price), and three of them were sold to a retailer (with sell-in price). After the sales, which began on the 7th of January 2016, the sell-out price was discounted for the 10% of the amount (35,10 euros). Seven pieces of the same stock keeping unit were then bought by another customer (with discounted sell-out price). On the 15th of January, so as to satisfies the selling requirements during the sales, several pieces of the same stock keeping unit have been produced with a lower cogs (12 euros). 20 of those pieces were sold to a reseller with a lower sell-in price (19,20 euros).
